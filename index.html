Biography

ennkeypee is founder of Bicbuc, a community driven online bookhub for students for making their Academic Life Easy, and leads the Keepin Softwares being a team member in each of its team. ennkeypee works for making life easy and himself choose tje same tools. He works on Ruby, Haml, Sass, Coffee-script and Datamapper. Also he regularly follows Opalrb and Rom-rb development.

While studying IT at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University at Delhi, ennkeypee felt of doing something for students so to make their daily life interesting and creative without harming their scores in exams. He still lives in Delhi and has hometown and office in Dehradun, a beautiful natural city in Uttarakhand, India.
